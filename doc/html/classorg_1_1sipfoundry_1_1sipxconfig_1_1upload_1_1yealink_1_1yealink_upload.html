Подробное описание

A Yealink files uploading class.

Used for various Yealink files uploading, such as: Firmware binaries LCD localization file(should be UTF32BE encoded) Idle Screen XML(for SIP-T38G only) Logo images for SIP-T1X and SIP-T2x models in DOB format
